FAQs

  1. How to handle a rattlesnake on my property?
    Cochise County non-emergency dispatch at 520-803-3555. Also Bill Schumacher out of Palominas is a local snake handler who can be reached at 520-227-3597.
  2. Local police contact numbers
    Cochise County non-emergency dispatch at 520-803-3555. Call 911 for emergencies.
  3. How do I get a gate code?
    Gate are controlled by 3 Canyons HOA. Please go to https://www.threecanyonsranch.com/contact for contact information.
